About This Data

Dissolved iron (DFe) is a crucial micronutrient for marine phytoplankton growth. The measurements shown here combine:

  • Direct DFe measurements from water samples
  • Remote sensing reflectance (Rrs_443) which can indicate phytoplankton activity

Key observations:

  • Surface waters typically show lower DFe due to biological uptake
  • Deeper waters often have higher DFe from remineralization
  • Seasonal patterns may indicate biological cycles
